DESCRIPTION

DTC No. Detection Item DTC Detection Condition Trouble Area
C054016 Brake Pressure Sensor "A" Circuit Voltage Below Threshold Master cylinder pressure is below -0.744 MPa (-7.6 kgf/cm2, -107 psi) for 5 seconds or more. Master cylinder pressure sensor (Brake actuator assembly)
C05401C Brake Pressure Sensor "A" Circuit Voltage Out of Range When vehicle speed is 7 km/h (4.3 mph) or more, braking is performed (stop light switch assembly is ON) and master cylinder pressure differential value is below 20 MPa/s, difference between master cylinder pressure estimated from vehicle deceleration and master cylinder pressure sensor input value is large for 1 second or more 5 times or more for 9 trips. Master cylinder pressure sensor (Brake actuator assembly)
C05401F Brake Pressure Sensor "A" Circuit Intermittent When vehicle speed is 10 km/h (6 mph) or more, noise in master cylinder pressure value occurs 7 times or more in 5 seconds. Master cylinder pressure sensor (Brake actuator assembly)
C054029 Brake Pressure Sensor "A" Signal Invalid Master cylinder pressure sensor invalid condition continues for 0.2 seconds or more. Master cylinder pressure sensor (Brake actuator assembly)
C05402A Brake Pressure Sensor "A" Signal Stuck In Range Any of the following is detected:
·

When vehicle speed is 7 km/h (4.3 mph) or more and master cylinder pressure exceeds 1.764 MPa (18.0 kgf/cm2, 255 psi), master cylinder pressure does not increase 0.048 MPa (0.5 kgf/cm2, 6.9 psi) or more continuously for 60 seconds.

·

When vehicle speed is 7 km/h (4.3 mph) and master cylinder pressure is 2.45 MPa (25.0 kgf/ cm2, 355 psi) or more, master cylinder pressure does not increase 0.048 MPa (0.5 kgf/cm2, 6.9 psi) or more continuously for 30 seconds.

·

Master cylinder pressure does not increase 0.048 MPa (0.5 kgf/cm2, 6.9 psi) or more 5 times or more while vehicle speed decreases from 40 km/h (25 mph) to 0 km/h (0 mph).


 Master cylinder pressure sensor (Brake actuator assembly)
C054031 Brake Pressure Sensor "A" No Signal Master cylinder pressure sensor communication invalid condition continues for 0.1 seconds or more. Master cylinder pressure sensor (Brake actuator assembly)
C054049 Brake Pressure Sensor "A" Internal Electronic Failure Any of the following is detected:
·

When voltage at terminal +BS is 9.5 V or higher, power supply voltage of master cylinder pressure sensor is continuously low for 1 second or more.

·

When master cylinder pressure sensor power supply voltage has risen, master cylinder pressure sensor has overheated or overcurrent of master cylinder pressure sensor continues for 1.2 seconds or more.


 Master cylinder pressure sensor (Brake actuator assembly)
C054096 Brake Pressure Sensor "A" Component Internal Failure Either of the following is detected:
·

Master cylinder pressure exceeds 23.328 MPa (237.8 kgf/cm2, 3382 psi) continuously for 0.1 seconds.

·

Master cylinder temperature exceeds 150°C (302°F) continuously for 0.1 seconds.


 Master cylinder pressure sensor (Brake actuator assembly)
