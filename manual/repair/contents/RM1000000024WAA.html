DTC No. Detection Item DTC Detection Condition Trouble Area
C050C23 Left Rear Wheel Speed Sensor Signal Stuck Low Either of the following is detected:
1.

When the +BS terminal voltage is 17.4 V or less at a vehicle speed of 10 km/h (6 mph) or more, output voltage from one of the speed sensors is less than that from the other sensors for 15 seconds or more.

2.

When the +BS terminal voltage is 17.4 V or less at a vehicle speed of 10 km/h (6 mph) or more, output from one of the speed sensors is 0 km/h (0 mph) for 1 second or more.

3.

When the +BS terminal voltage is 17.4 V or less at a vehicle speed of less than 10 km/h (6 mph) or more, outputs from both rear speed sensors are 0 km/h (0 mph) for 30 seconds or more.
·

Rear speed sensor LH

·

Sensor installation

·

Wire harness or connector

·

Skid control sensor wire

·

Rear skid control rotor LH (Rear axle hub and bearing assembly LH)
