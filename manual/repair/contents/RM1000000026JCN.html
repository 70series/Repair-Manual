BRAKE CONTROL / DYNAMIC CONTROL SYSTEMS  VEHICLE STABILITY CONTROL SYSTEM(w/ Pre-collision System)  P057113  Brake Switch "A" Circuit Open  


DESCRIPTION

The skid control ECU (brake actuator assembly) detects the brake operating conditions through a signal transmitted by the stop light switch.

The skid control ECU incorporates a circuit to detect an open circuit. This DTC is output when an open circuit is detected in the stop light signal input line.

DTC No. Detection Item DTC Detection Condition Trouble Area
P057113 Brake Switch "A" Circuit Open When the +BS terminal voltage is 9.5 V or higher, the stop light drive output (STPO) is OFF continuously for 0.1 seconds or more and there is an open circuit between A and B in the STP terminal circuit continuously for 0.3 seconds or more.
