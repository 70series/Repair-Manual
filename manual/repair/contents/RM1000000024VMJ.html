PRE-COLLISION  PRE-COLLISION SYSTEM  C142571  Stop Light Relay Actuator Stuck  


DESCRIPTION

The skid control ECU (brake actuator assembly) sends a stop light operation request signal to the stop light control relay (stop light switch assembly). When the skid control ECU (brake actuator assembly) detects a malfunction relating to the stop light circuit, the forward recognition camera is notified via CAN communication, and C142571 is output.

DTC No. Detection Item DTC Detection Condition Trouble Area
C142571 Stop Light Relay Actuator Stuck Once 3 seconds elapse after turning the ignition switch to ON, the system receives a stop light circuit malfunction signal from the skid control ECU (brake actuator assembly) for approximately 1 second or more. Vehicle stability control system

PROCEDURE
