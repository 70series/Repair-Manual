When any of the speed sensors or the yaw rate and acceleration sensor (airbag ECU assembly) has trouble, DTCs for the steering sensor may be output even when the steering sensor is normal. When DTCs for the speed sensors or yaw rate and acceleration sensor (airbag ECU assembly) are output together with DTCs for the steering sensor, inspect and repair the speed sensor and yaw rate and acceleration sensor (airbag ECU assembly) first, and then inspect and repair the steering sensor.
