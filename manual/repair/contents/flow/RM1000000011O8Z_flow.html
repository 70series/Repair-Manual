·

·

After replacing an injector, the ECM needs registration (See page Engine / Hybrid System>1VD-FTV (ENGINE CONTROL)>ECD SYSTEM>REGISTRATION 201408,999999,_51,_029760,_0135360,RM100000000FJKB, ).
