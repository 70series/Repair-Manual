CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

NOTICE:
·

After replacing the fuel supply pump, the ECM needs initialization (See page Engine / Hybrid System>1VD-FTV (ENGINE CONTROL)>ECD SYSTEM>INITIALIZATION 201408,999999,_51,_029760,_0135360,RM100000000FJIX, ).

·

After replacing an injector, the ECM needs registration (See page Engine / Hybrid System>1VD-FTV (ENGINE CONTROL)>ECD SYSTEM>REGISTRATION 201408,999999,_51,_029760,_0135360,RM100000000FJKB, ).



HINT:
·

This troubleshooting procedure checks for knocking and rattling.

·

Knocking is most likely to occur while the engine is idling.
