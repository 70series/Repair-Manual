NOTICE:

After replacing the ECM, the new ECM needs registration (See page Engine / Hybrid System>1VD-FTV (ENGINE CONTROL)>ECD SYSTEM>REGISTRATION 201408,999999,_51,_029760,_0135360,RM100000000FJKB, ) and initialization (See page Engine / Hybrid System>1VD-FTV (ENGINE CONTROL)>ECD SYSTEM>INITIALIZATION 201408,999999,_51,_029760,_0135360,RM100000000FJIX, ).


HINT:
·

After completing repairs, check that P0087, P0190, P0192 and/or P0193 is not set again.

·

If DTCs relating to different systems are output, and they share terminal E2 as their ground, check this ground circuit first.

·

Read freeze frame data using the GTS. Freeze frame data records the engine condition when malfunctions are detected. When troubleshooting, freeze frame data can help determine if the vehicle was moving or stationary, if the engine was warmed up or not, and other data from the time the malfunction occurred.
