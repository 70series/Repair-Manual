NOTICE:

After replacing an fuel injector, the ECM requires registration (Click here Engine / Hybrid System>1VD-FTV (ENGINE CONTROL)>ECD SYSTEM (w/o EGR System)>REGISTRATION 201701,999999,_51,_029760,_0309414,RM10000000136W1, ).


Explanation of Symptom


White Smoke When the engine is misfiring, any fuel that has been injected but remains unburned is emitted as white smoke.
Turbocharger internal oil leak (Click here ( 201701,999999,_51,_029760,_0309414,RM10000000136JT, ))
If oil leak occurs from turbine side seal, large amount of white smoke will be emitted from the exhaust pipe.
Internal oil leak is not visible from the outside of the turbocharger sub-assembly.
