Illuminate X2015 Front Recognition Camera Dirt History of forward recognition camera being unable to recognize the front and being temporarily unavailable

Check for the following

-

Front wiper blade other than genuine part may be attached

-

Front wiper rubber may be deteriorated

-

There may be stickers, accessories, baggage, etc. in visual field range of forward recognition camera

-

There may be snow, ice, fog, dirt, frost, dust or oil film sticking on windshield glass in front of camera

-

There may be damage or cracks on windshield glass in front of camera

-

Windshield glass other than genuine part may be installed

-

Confirm the vehicle was not driven in poor weather (the warning display will disappear when the conditions [darkness, backlight, snow, fog, etc.] around the forward recognition camera improve and the forward recognition camera can be used again)



Cause:

When foreign matter or dirt is attached to area around forward recognition camera
