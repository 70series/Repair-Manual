2.REMOVE FRONT BRAKE SHOE (w/o ABS)

a.

Remove the shoe hold down spring cup, shoe hold down spring, shoe hold down spring pin and front brake shoe.


b.

Using pliers, disconnect the parking brake wire LH from the parking brake shoe lever.

a.

47570 12.REMOVE REAR WHEEL BRAKE CYLINDER ASSEMBLY LH

a.

13.REMOVE REAR WHEEL BRAKE CYLINDER CUP

a.

Remove the 2 cylinder boots from the rear wheel brake cylinder assembly LH.


b.

Remove the 2 pistons and compression spring.


c.

Remove the 2 rear wheel brake cylinder cups from each piston.
