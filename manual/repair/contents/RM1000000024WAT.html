DESCRIPTION

The skid control ECU (brake actuator assembly) outputs this DTC when it receives a sensor power source malfunction signal from the steering sensor.

DTC No. Detection Item DTC Detection Condition Trouble Area
C14FE14 Steering Angle Sensor Supply Voltage Circuit Short to Ground or Open Steering sensor power supply malfunction signal is received from steering sensor.
·

Steering sensor (Spiral cable with sensor sub-assembly)

·

Wire harness or connector

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

NOTICE:
·

When replacing the steering sensor, confirm that the replacement part is of the correct specification.

·

Inspect the fuses for circuits related to this system before performing the following inspection procedure.



HINT:
·

When U007488, U012587 and/or U012687 is output together with C14FE14, inspect and repair the trouble areas indicated by U007488, U012587 and/or U012687 first.
