BRAKE CONTROL / DYNAMIC CONTROL SYSTEMS  VEHICLE STABILITY CONTROL SYSTEM(w/ Pre-collision System)  C137BA2  Brake System Control Module "A" System Voltage System Voltage Low  


DESCRIPTION

If a malfunction is detected in the power supply circuit, the skid control ECU (brake actuator assembly) stores this DTC and the fail-safe function prohibits ABS operation.

This DTC is stored when the +BS terminal voltage deviates from the DTC detection condition due to a malfunction in the power supply or charging circuit such as the battery or generator circuit, etc.

The DTC is canceled when the +BS terminal voltage returns to normal.

DTC No. Detection Item DTC Detection Condition Trouble Area
C137BA2 Brake System Control Module "A" System Voltage System Voltage Low Any of the following is detected:
·

When the +BS terminal voltage is below 9.5 V, the power source voltage for all speed sensors is continuously low for 60 seconds or more.

·

After the +BS terminal voltage is below 9.5 V continuously for 3 seconds or more, the +BS terminal voltage is below 9.5 V continuously for 7 seconds or more at a vehicle speed exceeding 3 km/h (2 mph).

·

When the +BS terminal voltage is 9.5 V or higher and the vehicle speed exceeds 15 km/h (9 mph), the IG1 terminal voltage is 10 V or less continuously for 60 seconds or more.

·

When the +BS terminal voltage is below 9.5 V and the solenoid relay is ON, the relay contact is OFF continuously for 1.22 seconds or more.

·

When the +BS terminal voltage is below 9.5 V, motor fail-safe relay overcurrent continues for 1.05 seconds or more.
