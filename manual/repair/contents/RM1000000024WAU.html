BRAKE CONTROL / DYNAMIC CONTROL SYSTEMS  VEHICLE STABILITY CONTROL SYSTEM(w/ Pre-collision System)  P057111  Brake Switch "A" Circuit Short to Ground  


DESCRIPTION

The skid control ECU (brake actuator assembly) inputs the stop light signal and brake operation condition. When the brake pedal is depressed and the stop light switch signal is not input, P057111 is output.

DTC No. Detection Item DTC Detection Condition Trouble Area
P057111 Brake Switch "A" Circuit Short to Ground After the master cylinder pressure exceeds 1.764 MPa (18.0 kgf/cm2, 255 psi) continuously for 5 seconds or more at a vehicle speed below 3 km/h (2 mph) with the stop light switch assembly off, the master cylinder pressure exceeds 1.764 MPa (18.0 kgf/cm2, 255 psi) continuously for 1 second or more when the vehicle speed decelerates from over 35 km/h (22 mph) to 3 km/h (2 mph) with the stop light switch assembly off.
