BRAKE CONTROL / DYNAMIC CONTROL SYSTEMS  VEHICLE STABILITY CONTROL SYSTEM(w/ Pre-collision System)  C14D71C  Multi-axis Acceleration Sensor Module "A" Supply Voltage Circuit Voltage Out of Range  


DESCRIPTION

The skid control ECU (brake actuator assembly) receives signals from the yaw rate and acceleration sensor (airbag ECU assembly) via the CAN communication system.

The airbag ECU assembly has a built-in acceleration sensor and detects the vehicle condition.

If there is trouble in the bus lines between the acceleration sensor (airbag ECU assembly) and the CAN communication system, the DTC U012587 is output.

These DTCs are also output when calibration has not been completed.

DTC No. Detection Item DTC Detection Condition Trouble Area
C14D71C Multi-axis Acceleration Sensor Module "A" Supply Voltage Circuit Voltage Out of Range When the +BS terminal voltage is from 9.5 to 17.4 V at a vehicle speed of more than 3 km/h (2 mph), the acceleration sensor power source malfunction signal is received for 10 seconds or more.
