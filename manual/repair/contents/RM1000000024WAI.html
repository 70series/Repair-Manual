BRAKE CONTROL / DYNAMIC CONTROL SYSTEMS  VEHICLE STABILITY CONTROL SYSTEM(w/ Pre-collision System)  C054017  Brake Pressure Sensor "A" Circuit Voltage Above Threshold  


DESCRIPTION

DTC No. Detection Item DTC Detection Condition Trouble Area
C054017 Brake Pressure Sensor "A" Circuit Voltage Above Threshold When vehicle speed is 3 km/h (2 mph) or more and stop light switch assembly is OFF, master cylinder pressure continuously exceeds 1.764 MPa (18.0 kgf/cm2, 255 psi) for 5 seconds or more.
·

Stop light switch assembly

·

Wire harness or connector

·

Skid control ECU (Brake actuator assembly)

·

Master cylinder pressure sensor (Brake actuator assembly)
