1VD-FTV ENGINE CONTROL  ECD SYSTEM  ECM Power Source Circuit  


DESCRIPTION

When the ignition switch is turned ON, the battery voltage is applied to the IGSW of the ECM. The output signal from the MREL terminal of the ECM causes a current to flow to the coil, closing the contacts of the EFI MAIN1 relay and supplying power to terminal +B of the ECM.
