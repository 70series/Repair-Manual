DTC No. Detection Item DTC Detection Condition Trouble Area
C007800 Different Diameter Tire The vehicle speed exceeds 20 km/h (12 mph) and a difference exceeding 20% between the value output from the speed sensors of the front wheels and the rear wheels occurs the specified number of times or more.

·
