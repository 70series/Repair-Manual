DESCRIPTION

The skid control ECU (brake actuator assembly) receives the signals from the ECM, steering and yaw rate and acceleration sensor (airbag ECU assembly) via the CAN communication system.

DTC No. Detection Item DTC Detection Condition Trouble Area
U007488 Control Module Communication Bus "B" Off Bus Off Either of the following is detected:
1.

A bus OFF state occurs once or more within 0.1 seconds, 10 times in succession. (Sent signals cannot be received.)

2.

When the +BS terminal voltage is between 9.5 and 17.4 V, a delay in receiving data from the yaw rate sensor and steering sensor continues for 1 second or more.

3.

When the +BS terminal voltage is between 9.5 and 17.4 V, the following occurs 10 times in succession within 60 seconds.

-

The condition that a delay in receiving data from the yaw rate sensor and steering sensor occurs more than once within 5 seconds.
