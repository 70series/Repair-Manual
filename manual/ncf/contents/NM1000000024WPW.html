CONSTRUCTION


a.

The camera heater is composed of the sheet heater (heating element) and temperature fuse, etc. Current is run through the sheet heater (heating element) to generate heat and warm the front portion of the forward recognition camera.

OPERATION


a.

When all of the following conditions have been met, the camera heater operates.

·

The ignition switch is turned ON.

·

The ambient temperature is 10°C (50°F) or less.

·

30 seconds or more have elapsed since the camera heater operation stopped.


b.

After the camera heater operates, the operation will stop when a certain length of time has elapsed.

HINT:

Operating time will differ based on vehicle speed, auxiliary battery voltage and ambient temperature.
