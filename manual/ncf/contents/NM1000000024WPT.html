OPERATION


a.

If the forward recognition camera determines that the possibility of a collision is high, it sends a signal to the combination meter assembly. Upon receiving this signal, the combination meter assembly indicates a warning using the PCS activation indicator light and buzzer. Details are indicated below.

·

When there is a high possibility of a collision.

PCS Activation Indicator Light Buzzer
Flashes Sounds Continuously



b.

When the system is not available, the following warning occurs to warn the driver.

·

When a system malfunction is detected.

Detail PCS Warning Light Buzzer
When the forward recognition camera detects a system malfunction. Illuminates Sounds

·

Millimeter wave radar sensor assembly low voltage malfunction

·

Millimeter wave radar sensor assembly temperature malfunction

·

Dirty center mark of upper radiator grille or millimeter wave radar sensor assembly

·

Millimeter wave radar sensor assembly misaligned

·

Abnormal forward recognition camera voltage

·

Other forward recognition camera malfunction

·

Forward recognition camera temperature outside of operational range

·

Dirty or fogged windshield glass over the forward recognition camera
